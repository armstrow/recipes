Potato: Baked Potato Topping

1 pint sour cream
4 cups grated cheese (16 ounce)
1 cup butter, softened
¼ cup green onions or chives
1 teaspoon salt
Potato: Barbecue Style Potatoes

6 medium unpeeled potatoes cut into 4 wedges
1/3 cup margarine, melted
¼ cup catsup
2 teaspoon prepared mustard
½ teaspoon paprika
½ teaspoon salt

Cut potatoes crosswise at ¼ inch intervals to within ¼ inch from bottom of potatoes. Place in ice water 1 hour, and then dry. Mix rest of ingredients. Place potatoes on baking sheet. Brush with half the catsup mixture. Bake in 425° oven, brushing occasionally with remaining mixture, for 35 minutes or until tender.
Potato: Nauvoo (Funeral) Potatoes

6-7 good-sized potatoes
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream (8 oz.)
½ cup melted butter
¾ cup grated cheese
¼ cup minced onion
bread crumbs or corn flakes
2 tablespoons butter

Boil, cool, peed, and shred potatoes into a casserole (microwaving works too). Blend soup, sour cream, melted butter, cheese, and onion. Cover potatoes with sauce. Top with bread crumbs combined with butter. Bake at 350º for 30 minutes.
Potato: Rosemary Potatoes

1 1/2 pounds small red potatoes
1 tablespoon corn starch
1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
or 2 teaspoons dried rosemary
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Preheat oven to 450.  Rinse and cut potatoes into 1 inch pieces.  Place potatoes and spices into ziploc bag and shake well to coat.  Spread to a single layer in baking pan and bake for 30 minutes, stirring when half done.
Sweet Potatoes

6 pounds sweet potatoes (fill 6 quart pressure cooker)
3 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice or (since I never have pumpkin pie spice):
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon ginger

Topping
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup chopped pecans
3 tablespoons melted butter

Boil potatoes until just tender, then drain and mash with spices.  Put in 9x13 baking dish.  Mix topping ingredients together and spread over potatoes.  Heat in 400 degree oven for 25 minutes
