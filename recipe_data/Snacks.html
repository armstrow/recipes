Mustard Sauce

¼ cup mustard
¼ cup vinegar
¼ cup sugar
1 egg
¼ teaspoon salt
½ cup mayonnaise

Mix mustard and vinegar, let stand overnight.
Add sugar and egg. Cook stirring constantly until thick. Remove from heat, add salt. Cool and add mayonnaise.
Sweet Chex Mix

1 large box Corn Chex
1 small box Golden Grahams
8 oz. Shredded coconut
1 ½ cup sliced almonds
2 cups white corn syrup
1 ½ cup margarine
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla

Mix cereals, coconut, and almonds in large bowl.
Bring other ingredients to boil and boil for 1 minute. Pour over cereal and stir until coated. Lay on wax paper to dry.
Sugared Corn Pops

1 pound butter
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup light Karo
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon soda
1 teaspoon vanilla (optional)
3 (7-9 oz.) bags of corn pops

Heat butter, sugar, Karo, and salt to boiling. Remove from heat, add soda and vanilla. Pour over corn pops.
Sugared Corn Pops (variation)

1 1/3 cubes butter
2/3 cups sugar
2 tablespoons water
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 (5 oz.) bag of corn pops

Melt butter in pan, add sugar and water. Bring to boil. Boil fast for 4 minutes. Remove from heat, add vanilla. Pour over corn pops and stir until coated. Spread on cookie sheet. Place in oven at 225º for 10 minutes. Remove and cool.
Bugles and Almonds

1 cup butter
2 tablespoons light Karo
1 cup sugar
¼ cup water
2 boxes (1 large bag) Bugles
1 package sliced almonds or cashews

Boil to soft ball. Pour over Bugles. Add sliced almonds or cashews.
