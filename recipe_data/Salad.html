Frog Eye Salad (Grandma Armstrong)

1 cup uncooked Acini de Pepe pasta
½ cup sugar
1 tablespoon flour
½ cup pineapple juice
Juice from 1 lemon
1 egg yolk
1 cup whipping cream, whipped
1 can crushed pineapple
1 can mandarin oranges, cut into pieces
½ small package of miniature marshmallows

Cook Acini de Pepe pasta in water, drain, and let cool. Mix together sugar and flour in saucepan. Add pineapple and lemon juice and egg yolk and beat well. Heat to boiling for 5 minutes, then cool. Add whipped cream, then rushed pineapple, oranges, and marshmallows.
Frozen Fruit (Grandma Armstrong)

½ cup sugar
1 tablespoon flour
½ cup pineapple juice
Juice from 1 lemon
1 egg yolk
1 cup whipping cream, whipped
2 ½ cup fruit cocktail
1 small bottle of maraschino cherries
1 can pineapple tidbits or chunks
3 sliced bananas
1 cup mandarin oranges

Mix together sugar and flour in saucepan. Add pineapple and lemon juice and egg yolk and beat well. Heat to boiling for 5 minutes, then cool. Add whipped cream, fruit cocktail (note: pick the grapes out and substitute them with fresh grapes for a better flavor), cherries, pineapple chunks, bananas, and mandarin oranges. Pour into 9x13 banking pan a freeze for 24 hours.
Spinach Salad

1 package baby spinach (10-12 oz.)
1 head romaine lettuce
1 cup strawberries, sliced
¼ cup dried cranberries (craisins)
1 can mandarin oranges
1 small rend onion, sliced thin
¼ cup sliced almonds
3 tablespoons sugar
1 bottle Brianna’s Poppy Seed Dressing

Put almonds and sugar in small pan. Cook over medium heat until sugar dissolves and coats almonds, stirring constantly. Pour onto wax paper to cool. Toss ingredients together.
Sweet Cucumber Salad

10 medium cucumbers
1 small red onion, chopped
2 teaspoons salt
¾ cup white vinegar
¾ cup sugar
1/3 cup fresh dill or chives, finely chopped
½ teaspoon white pepper
¼ teaspoon ground celery seeds

Wash cucumbers under very hot water to remove wax coating, remove ends and thinly slice. Toss onion with salt and cucumber. Press in colander to remove as much juice as possible. Transfer cucumbers and onions back to large bowl. Toss with remaining ingredients. Pack tightly in quart jar until serving. Makes 8 servings.
